Tap-Dance Help

    Base field accepts free text (e.g. TD(3)).

    Combo Keycodes

    Keys field: Enter characters or keycodes separated by commas (e.g. "J, K" or "KC_J, KC_K").

    Result field: Enter a keycode, use Quantum Keycode format, or Layer functions:

    • Simple: ESC, KC_ESC, Bksp
    • Quantum format: Alt+Bksp, Ctrl+Shift+A, Cmd+C
    • Layer functions: MO(1), TG(2), TO(3), TT(1), OSL(2)
    • Supported modifiers: Alt, Ctrl, Shift, Cmd (or Win, Super)
    • Right modifiers: RAlt, RCtrl, RShift, RCmd

    Quantum Keycodes are automatically converted to QMK format (e.g. Alt+Bksp → LALT(KC_BSPC)).

    Layer Functions:

    • MO(layer) - Momentary Layer (activates layer while held)
    • TG(layer) - Toggle Layer (toggles layer on/off)
    • TO(layer) - Turn On Layer (activates layer permanently)
    • TT(layer) - Tap Toggle Layer (tap = toggle, hold = momentary)
    • OSL(layer) - One Shot Layer (activates layer for one keypress sequence)

    You can also use formats like MO1 or MO 1 instead of MO(1).

    Supported Keycodes

    Letters & Numbers: A-Z, 0-9 (or KC_A - KC_Z, KC_0 - KC_9)

    Special Keys:

    • SPACE or SPC → KC_SPC
    • ENTER or ENT → KC_ENT
    • TAB → KC_TAB
    • ESC or ESCAPE → KC_ESC
    • BACKSPACE, BSPC, or BKSP → KC_BSPC
    • DELETE or DEL → KC_DEL

    Arrow Keys:

    • UP → KC_UP
    • DOWN → KC_DOWN
    • LEFT → KC_LEFT
    • RIGHT → KC_RIGHT
    • HOME → KC_HOME
    • END → KC_END
    • PAGEUP or PGUP → KC_PGUP
    • PAGEDOWN or PGDN → KC_PGDN

    Modifiers:

    • CTRL, LCTRL, or LCTL → KC_LCTL
    • SHIFT, LSHIFT, or LSFT → KC_LSFT
    • ALT or LALT → KC_LALT
    • CMD, WIN, SUPER, LGUI, LCMD, or LWIN → KC_LGUI
    • RCTRL or RCTL → KC_RCTL
    • RSHIFT or RSFT → KC_RSFT
    • RALT → KC_RALT
    • RGUI, RCMD, or RWIN → KC_RGUI

    Function Keys: F1 - F24 → KC_F1 - KC_F24

    Punctuation & Symbols:

    • - or MINUS → KC_MINS
    • = or EQUAL → KC_EQL
    • [ or LBRACKET → KC_LBRC
    • ] or RBRACKET → KC_RBRC
    • \ or BACKSLASH → KC_BSLS
    • ; or SEMICOLON → KC_SCLN
    • ' or QUOTE → KC_QUOT
    • ` or GRAVE → KC_GRV
    • , or COMMA → KC_COMM
    • . or DOT → KC_DOT
    • / or SLASH → KC_SLSH

    Media Keys:

    • PLAY or MPLY → KC_MPLY
    • PAUSE → KC_MPLY (same as play in QMK)
    • STOP or MSTP → KC_MSTP
    • NEXT, MNXT, or NEXTTrack → KC_MNXT
    • PREVIOUS, MPRV, PREV, or PREVIOUSTRACK → KC_MPRV
    • FASTFORWARD, MFFD, or FFD → KC_MFFD
    • REWIND, MRWD, or RWD → KC_MRWD

    Mac-Specific Keys:

    • MACPLAY → KC_MPLY
    • MACPAUSE → KC_MPLY
    • MACNEXT → KC_MNXT
    • MACPREVIOUS or MACPREV → KC_MPRV
    • MacSSV+ or MACSSV+ → LSA(KC__VOLUP) (Mac Small Step Volume Up)
    • MacSSV- or MACSSV- → LSA(KC__VOLDOWN) (Mac Small Step Volume Down)

    MacSSV (Mac Small Step Volume) provides finer volume control on macOS with smaller increments than standard volume keys. SS stands for "Small Step".

    Other Keys:

    • CAPS or CAPSLOCK → KC_CAPS
    • NUMLOCK → KC_NUM
    • SCROLLLOCK → KC_SCRL

    Note: You can also use the QMK keycode format directly (e.g. KC_BSPC instead of BACKSPACE).
